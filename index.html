Shopping List

  • 1 lb. Chicken Breast
  • 1 Dozen Eggs
  • 10oz. Bread crumbs.
  • Aluminum foil.
  • 1/4 lb. Cheese

Chicken Parm Recipe üêî

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees (175 degrees C).
  2. Place bread crumbs and egg in a bowl. Mix.
  3. Dip Chicken into mix. Then place on pan.
  4. Wrap in aluminum foil.
  5. Cook for 40 minutes.
  6. Add sauce and serve.
